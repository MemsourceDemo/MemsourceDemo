    Translation Management

    Memsource offers a translation solution for everyone. Whether you yourself translate or choose to outsource your translation, Memsource can help you make the process more efficient. Some of the world’s largest translation agencies and enterprise translation departments rely on Memsource to optimize their translation productivity.

    Translation Environment

    Memsource Web Editor is a fully web-based translation environment. Its desktop alternative, Memsource Editor, is available as a free download. Both connect seamlessly to translation projects, translation memories and term bases in Memsource Cloud. Join tens of thousands of translators across the globe that happily translate using Memsource.

    Translation Analytics

    Powerful analytics help you analyze your translation process in real-time and stay on top of things. Analyze language volumes, file types, costing, and translation quality by vendor or machine translation usage. Examine the data directly in Memsource Cloud, export the data or integrate Memsource with your in-house systems via Memsource APIs.
